What is Arcade Commander?

Arcade Commander 2.0 is a Windows-based lighting platform built for arcade cabinets. It combines live control, effect authoring, game-specific mapping, and automation in one coordinated suite. With ArcadeCommanderV2, ACLighter, and ACDispatch working together, operators can design, test, and trigger lighting behavior with consistent results.
